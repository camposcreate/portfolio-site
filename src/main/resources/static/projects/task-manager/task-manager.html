Welcome to the Task Manager! organize, prioritize, and complete tasks.

Whether you're a list-maker or a goal-setter, this application is designed to streamline your workflow. Create, manage, and track your tasks.


How To Use

  • Utilize the input field to generate a new task.
  • Indicate whether your task is a priority if needed.
  • Click the 'add' button to instantly populate and view your personalized task list.
  • Remove tasks by selecting specific tasks and clicking 'Delete'.
  • For a clean slate, click 'Clear All' to delete all tasks and begin anew.
